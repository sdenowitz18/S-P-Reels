# sp-reels — Product Vision
_Last updated: 2026-05-04_

---

## What it is

sp-reels is a cinematic taste platform. It helps people understand their own taste in film and TV, share that taste with people they care about, and make better decisions about what to watch — alone or together.

The core insight: **ratings alone don't explain taste**. Two people can both give The Godfather 5 stars for completely different reasons. sp-reels goes deeper — across 12 cinematic dimensions (narrative legibility, emotional directness, scale, pacing, etc.), it maps what you actually respond to and why.

---

## North star

> "The most personally accurate film recommendation you've ever received — and a reason why that resonates."

---

## Principles

1. **Taste over titles.** We're not a watchlist app. The catalog is a tool for understanding preferences, not an end in itself.

2. **Signal over noise.** Every feature should sharpen the taste model or surface it more meaningfully. Features that don't contribute to this are lower priority.

3. **Honest over flattering.** Match scores reflect real alignment. A 62% is useful. Artificial inflation isn't.

4. **Social as context, not core.** The social layer makes taste more useful (shared decisions, discovery via friends) — but the primary relationship is the user with their own taste.

5. **Cinema and TV are equal.** Both mediums have dimensions worth tracking. The same taste model applies.

6. **Progressive depth.** The first-run experience should feel instant and rewarding. The full depth (all 12 dimensions, compatibility analysis, mood room) reveals itself over time.

---

## What sp-reels is not

- A social network (no feed, no likes, no follower counts)
- A streaming service aggregator (JustWatch handles that)
- A critic platform (user taste, not critical consensus)
- A recommendation engine that hides its reasoning (always show why)

---

## The five experiences

1. **Know your taste** — Your taste code, profile, dimension breakdown, quick rate
2. **Log what you watch** — Add/search, rate, interview, live notes
3. **Understand your people** — Compatibility, blend, friend profiles
4. **Decide what's next** — Catalog, mood room, recommendations
5. **Get started well** — Onboarding, calibration, import

---

## Long-term expansion

The taste model and dimension framework is designed to be portable. A natural extension is **sp-books** — the same system applied to literature, with book-appropriate dimensions, ratings from Goodreads/Open Library, and equivalent enrichment pipeline.
